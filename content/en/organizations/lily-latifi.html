body: >-

           Lily Latifi is a passionate textile designer with more
than 25 years' experience in creating tailor-made solutions for interior
design. She stand out by her unique way of blending discreet elegance with
(re)fined functionality.

Lily carefully selects only
the best performance materials to suit each project that she studies through to
the smallest detail. She combines an artisanal and innovative approach to create textile solutions that meet needs in terms of luminosity,
visibility, modularity, heat and acoustic comfort.

 

From the rigorous choice of French or German textiles
to manufacturing carried out entirely in France, Lily Latifi is committed to a
local and quality production of her works.

She is interested in all soft
materials : textiles, paper, foam... However, her favorite material is
100% natural wool felt, which she tenderly describes as her "creative
companion". She appreciates its sensuality, softness and acoustic and heat
regulating qualities, which make it an ideal medium for designing mobile
partitions, wall coverings and acoustic suspensions that are both aesthetically
pleasing and functional. Her works transform spaces : they gently separate,
conceal without weighing down, and create a soothing atmosphere without ever
sacrificing the beauty of the gesture.

Lily Latifi's organic and
minimal world is inspired by nature - starting with natural light, clouds and
bare landscapes - which she translates into light, poetic drawings or embossed
material prints. Thanks to her artistic sense and technical expertise, she
breathes fresh air into the often standardized world of interior textiles and
acoustics. Her creations don't just fulfill a function : they tell a story,
that of an art of living with finesse and harmony.
