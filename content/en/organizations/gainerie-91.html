For nearly 60 years, Gainerie 91 has been designing packaging and visual merchandising solutions for leading luxury brands. Its teams, spread across four production sites around the world, combine artisanal expertise, industrial engineering, and a commitment to sustainability.

body: >-


Gainerie 91 is the priviledged interlocutor of all the world famous jewellery, clock and spirits brands. The company's artisans create handcrafted products of the highest standard. Exceptional quality control at every step of the manufacturing process forms the basis for the reputation of excellence that Gainerie 91 has maintained for more than forty years in the areas of leatherwork, wood work, laquering, hot stamping, silkscreening, injection molding and metal cutting.  

Recover
Recover, Gainerie 91 Group's modular bust combines durability, elegance and innovation. Its clipsable base and interchangeable 3D textile dressing adapts to the seasons and brand worlds. Repairable, reusable and recyclable, Recover offers a responsible and flexible alternative for a durable luxury POS.
