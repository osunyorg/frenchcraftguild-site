Luxury packaging

body: >-


Gainerie 91 is the priviledged interlocutor of all the world famous jewellery, clock and spirits brands. The company's artisans create handcrafted products of the highest standard. Exceptional quality control at every step of the manufacturing process forms the basis for the reputation of excellence that Gainerie 91 has maintained for more than forty years in the areas of leatherwork, wood work, laquering, hot stamping, silkscreening, injection molding and metal cutting.
