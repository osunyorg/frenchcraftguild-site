From France with love

body: >-


After several years devoted to designing exceptional residences in the United States and Europe, Anne Carminati founded her interior textile brand-bed, bath, and table linens, throws, cushions, and embroidery on textiles-with a clear vision: to offer exclusive, made-to-order collections that celebrate the excellence of French craftsmanship. Rooted in a passion for fine materials and exceptional craftsmanship, Maison Anne Carminati celebrates textiles as a form of artistic and timeless expression. Designed in her Paris studio and crafted by French artisans with precision, her collections tell a story of refined textures and subtle color palettes inspired by French cultural heritage. Maison Anne Carminati embodies timeless, uncompromising luxury: bespoke craftsmanship, rare materials, and impeccable finishes, offering decorators and architects the freedom to personalize each creation, from design to embroidery.
