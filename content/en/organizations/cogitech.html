Aqua Muta
Cogitech proposes a dialogue between geometric rigor and the organic flow of water. How can this natural element alter or sculpt a defined structure? How can we give materiality to an image and sensitivity to a digital file? This methodology deploys a chain of transformation from the immaterial to the tangible, from digital language to the subtlety of gesture. By exploiting the random nature of the algorithm, each experiment generates a unique work. What is initially only a digital simulation is transformed into a sculpture: an infinity of solutions materializes in images, then in 3D files, and finally becomes tangible objects. The pieces presented are unique and bear witness to this transformation. Reworked by hand, Cogitech's artisans magnify the complexity of the forms with exceptional finishes. Each wave, each movement of the water is reappropriated by the hand of man, who emphasizes the object's dynamics by applying a deep chrome and a smoky varnish. These four vases are examples of this artistic research, a reflection on the fusion between digital manufacturing and 21st-century craftsmanship.
