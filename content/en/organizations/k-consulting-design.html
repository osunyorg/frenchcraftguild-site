The K+ Consulting & Design agency supports the actors who shape our living environment - contractors, designers, architects, engineers, craftsmen, manufacturers - to transform an intention into demanding achievements, both desirable and sustainable:

  • by building bridges between creation, technology, manufacturing and market so that your projects and solutions are achievable, viable and diffused,
  • by accelerating the transition from the concept to the construction site, from the unique piece to the collection, from the local to the international.

To the key: a prescriptible offer that becomes a reference, here and internationally.
