Inaugurated in 2021 in the heart of the Grande Écurie at the Palace of Versailles, the campus is both a network and a place to discover, practice, and train in heritage and craftsmanship professions. A non-profit association, Campus Versailles brings together and animates a community of public and private actors: education professionals, heritage companies, artisans, students, and institutions, who pool their resources to promote the professions, accelerate the upskilling of skills, and create an exceptional training system.
