The Versailles Campus is a network and a place where you can discover, practice, and train in heritage and craft professions. Inaugurated in 2021 in the heart of the Grande Écurie at the Palace of Versailles, it focuses on five areas of excellence: arts and crafts, built heritage, horticulture and landscaping, gastronomy, and tourism.
