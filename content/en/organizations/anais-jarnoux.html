Phycotex - Anaïs Jarnoux & Samuel Tomatis

PHYCOTEX is a flexible, biodegradable material made from seaweed, offering an alternative to traditional leathers and plastics. Combining science, design and craftsmanship, their approach is based on a circular economy model in which production waste is reintroduced into the manufacturing process. Lauded by the Liliane Bettencourt Prize for "intelligence of the hand"®.
