Jean-Baptiste Leca and Carlyne Sevestre founded Leca Sevestre Avocats to support creators and businesses in their innovative and creative projects, at the crossroads of design, technology and industry. The firm intervenes in intellectual property law and technological and commercial contracts. Used to advising actors with rare or innovative know-how, the firm secures every stage of the project, from creation to exploitation: protection of know-how, product development, industrialization, commercialization, exploitation, valorisation and disputes that can follow. Creative and committed, Jean-Baptiste and Carlyne design solutions that match your ambitions.
