Digital craftsmen

body: >-


After years of working for the most demanding brands, Arnaud Levy, Pierre-André Boissinot, Sébastien Gaya and Alexis Benoit decided to create a web development cooperative.To this business philosophy, already rare in the sector, is added a strong commitment to the values of excellence, ethics and education around creative and immersive digital productions.WebGL, 3D, virtual reality, augmented reality... so many technical approaches that aim to push back the limits of the classic Web, but with the full awareness that digital technology today generates its own share of pollution, notably through colossal energy consumption.Eco-designed sites are therefore at the heart of their offer, a genuine alternative that limits ecological impact and foreshadows the future of the profession. “The Web is also about discovery and emotion”. In approaching game design, and more generally interactive storytelling, the collective never forgets that this “10th art” is also, and above all, at the service of culture.

After years of working for the most demanding brands, Arnaud Levy, Pierre-André Boissinot, Sébastien Gaya and Alexis Benoit decided to create a web development cooperative.

To this business philosophy, already rare in the sector, is added a strong commitment to the values of excellence, ethics and education around creative and immersive digital productions.

WebGL, 3D, virtual reality, augmented reality... so many technical approaches that aim to push back the limits of the classic Web, but with the full awareness that digital technology today generates its own share of pollution, notably through colossal energy consumption.

Eco-designed sites are therefore at the heart of their offer, a genuine alternative that limits ecological impact and foreshadows the future of the profession. “The Web is also about discovery and emotion”. In approaching game design, and more generally interactive storytelling, the collective never forgets that this “10th art” is also, and above all, at the service of culture.
