Since 2022, the French Craft has been a partner of the "Rencontres des savoir-faire" (Know-how meetings). An understanding of materials, new plant-based sources, augmented craftsmanship... The lecture series is back with new speakers and even more expertise! The Rencontres are organized by the Manufactures nationales, the Campus Mode Métiers d'art Design-Manufactures des Gobelins, and ENSAD, with support from the Bettencourt Schueller Foundation and

Gestes

magazine .
