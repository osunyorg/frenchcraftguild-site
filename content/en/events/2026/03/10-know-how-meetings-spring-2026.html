body: >-

The program for this series of meetings focuses on innovative practices in the crafts.
The Manufactures Nationales set up a Research and Innovation Department, in which the Sustainable Practices Laboratory is being developed. Several areas of applied research will be implemented. One of these involves support for the development and promotion of eco-responsible materials. These materials tend towards the same ideal of respect for the environment, and have several characteristics: they are renewable, biodegradable or recyclable, and non-toxic. The Rencontres are an opportunity to present and better understand the issues surrounding these materials.
