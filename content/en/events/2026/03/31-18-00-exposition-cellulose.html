On March 31 at 6 p.m., the French Craft invites you to  Procédés Chénel to discover "a material experience" proposed by Hyloh in partnership MateriO . Between samples of materials and pieces of creators, explore cellulose in many forms: burnt wood (Shou Sugi Ban), wood shot by Wook, iridescent wood, ultra-performing paper composites (PaperShell), paper ceramics (Ludovic Brush), moulded cellulose sculptures (Frits Achten), bacterial cellulose, nanocellulose, hemp coatings (Procédés Chénel International), algae-based materials (Samuel Tomatis)... The session ended in a warm atmosphere around a moment of exchange at the showroom of Procédés Chénel.
Tuesday, 31 March 6 p.m. - 8 p.m. Procédés C hénel processes - 70 rue Jean Bleuzen - 92170 Vanves Metro line 13 Entrance into the building - Staircase C, elevator level 2
