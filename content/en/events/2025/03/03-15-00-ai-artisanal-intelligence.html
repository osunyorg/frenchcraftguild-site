French Craft joins forces with Entreprises du Patrimoine Vivant (EPV), members of the Réseau Excellence EPV association, to organize an afternoon of exchanges dedicated to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its formidable potential for the crafts of gesture and creation.  

body: >-


On March 3, 2025, the Procédés Chénel workshops, members of French Craft and EPV, will welcome Henri Danzin and Charles de Dainville (Maison Jaùh // French Craft), who will share their day-to-day experience of this multi-faceted tool. A live demonstration will explore each stage of the process and invite the audience to interact.Valentin Goux (Rinck // Réseau Excellence EPV) will explain how he is already integrating AI into his business, from furniture design to interior architecture, translation, content creation, photo retouching and much more.The event will close with a convivial moment from 6pm and the opening of the Paper Wonders exhibition, featuring prototype luminaires designed using paper solutions from Procédés Chénel.

On March 3, 2025, the Procédés Chénel (external link) workshops, members of French Craft and EPV, will welcome Henri Danzin and Charles de Dainville (Maison Jaùh (external link) // French Craft), who will share their day-to-day experience of this multi-faceted tool. A live demonstration will explore each stage of the process and invite the audience to interact.

Valentin Goux (Rinck (external link)// Réseau Excellence EPV (external link)) will explain how he is already integrating AI into his business, from furniture design to interior architecture, translation, content creation, photo retouching and much more.

The event will close with a convivial moment from 6pm and the opening of the Paper Wonders exhibition, featuring prototype luminaires designed using paper solutions from Procédés Chénel (external link).
