On the program for this series of meetings: new sustainable materials.
The Manufactures Nationales set up a Research and Innovation Department, in which the Sustainable Practices Laboratory is being developed. Several areas of applied research will be implemented. One of these involves support for the development and promotion of eco-responsible materials. These materials tend towards the same ideal of respect for the environment, and have several characteristics: they are renewable, biodegradable or recyclable, and non-toxic.This meeting is an opportunity to present and better understand the challenges of these materials, which are both innovative and at the heart of the challenges facing the craft industry.> Materials from the sea: industry and value chain - Tuesday November 26, 2024 at 6:30pm with Thomas Eberhard (Cuir de poisson), David Rosenblum (Atelier Bettenfeld-Rosenblum)), Maxime Roux (Ostrea Design)> Recovering value: the challenge of deposits - Tuesday December 17, 2024 at 6:30pm with Nativité Rodriguez (Atelier des Matières) et Laurent Corio (Agence Les Nouvelles)> Cultural Matter(s) - Tuesday, January 28, 2025 at 6:30 pm with Antonin Mongin, Artisan-designer textile, et Vincent Lartizien (Les Chanvres de l'Atlantique)

The Manufactures Nationales set up a Research and Innovation Department, in which the Sustainable Practices Laboratory is being developed. Several areas of applied research will be implemented. One of these involves support for the development and promotion of eco-responsible materials. These materials tend towards the same ideal of respect for the environment, and have several characteristics: they are renewable, biodegradable or recyclable, and non-toxic.

This meeting is an opportunity to present and better understand the challenges of these materials, which are both innovative and at the heart of the challenges facing the craft industry.
