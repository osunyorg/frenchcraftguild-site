Return to Materials & Light with two days of exhibitions, conferences and workshops on the theme of innovative materials for architecture and design. New for the 8th edition: M&L CRAFT, an exhibition and workshop series on the theme of exceptional materials and know-how.

body: >-


French Craft was delighted to partner the Capsule Craft presented at Materials & Light, September 12 & 13, 2022 at the Carreau du Temple. Materials & Light is a trade event dedicated to specifiers and designers, organized by d'a magazine in partnership with muuuz. Traditional materials revisited, innovative assemblies, new approaches... discover the designers selected for this capsule presented exclusively and also find the summary of the round-table discussion “From workshop to factory, what developments for handcrafted-designed solutions?” (in French).

From the workshop to the factory: what developments for hand-crafted solutions?
Round table moderated by Magali Lancien (French Craft Guild) and Christophe Poble (Le Lab) with Philippe Badaroux (President of Cluster Lumière), Laurent Corio (Designer), Yann Santerre (Founder of Gwilen), Arnaud Bousquet-Ky (Director at Matériaupôle Paris Seine-Amont), Nicolas Dubois (Ateliers Roméo), Philippe Tissot (Co-founder at KoMuT).
Wednesday, September 13, 11 a.m. • Conferences area
