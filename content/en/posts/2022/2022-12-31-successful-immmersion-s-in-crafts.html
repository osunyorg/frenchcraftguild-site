Located in the heart of the Marais, IMMERSION/S welcomed from 15 to 17 October twenty artists and artisans with unique know-how, through high-tech works.

body: >-


An initiative of FAFCEA (Training Insurance Fund for Craft Business Owners), whose ambition was to shake up the codes of existing trade shows and convey a different vision of fine crafts., oriented towards the creation of new pieces. On more than 900 m2, the EXCELLSENS team deployed a cutting-edge selection of exhibitors who were able to demonstrate their virtuosity in a unique atmosphere, with new video installations, a charitable auction and a round table on the theme of innovation to which the French Craft was pleased to intervene. Below is the replay in French of the round table "Innovation and Crafts of Art" with Anahita Vessier (The Winners of Intelligence of the Hand); Sébastien Daix (FAFCEA); Christophe Poissonnier (Villes et Métiers d ́art), André Fontes and Guillaume Lehoux (Studio Noir Vif) and Magali Lancien (French Craft Guild).

An initiative of FAFCEA (Training Insurance Fund for Craft Business Owners), whose ambition was to shake up the codes of existing trade shows and convey a different vision of fine crafts., oriented towards the creation of new pieces. On more than 900 m2, the EXCELLSENS team deployed a cutting-edge selection of exhibitors who were able to demonstrate their virtuosity in a unique atmosphere, with new video installations, a charitable auction and a round table on the theme of innovation to which the French Craft was pleased to intervene.

Below is the replay in French of the round table "Innovation and Crafts of Art" with Anahita Vessier (The Winners of Intelligence of the Hand); Sébastien Daix (FAFCEA); Christophe Poissonnier (Villes et Métiers d ́art), André Fontes and Guillaume Lehoux (Studio Noir Vif) and Magali Lancien (French Craft Guild).
