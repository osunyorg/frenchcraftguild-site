On March 3, 2025, the  Procédés Chénel  workshops, members of the French Craft and EPV, welcomed Henri Danzin and Charles de Dainville ( Maison Jaùh  // French Craft), to share their day-to-day experience of this multi-faceted tool. A live demonstration explored each stage of the process, in interaction with the audience. Valentin Goux ( Rinck  //  Réseau Excellence EPV ) then showed how he was already integrating AI into his business, from furniture design to interior architecture, translation, content creation, photo retouching and much more. The event ended with a convivial moment in the Procédés Chénel showroom. Watch JAÙH's webconference from this masterclass below.
