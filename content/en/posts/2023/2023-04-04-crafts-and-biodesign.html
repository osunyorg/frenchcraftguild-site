On the occasion of the European Art Trades Day (JEMA (external link)) to be held from 27 March to 3 April 2023 on the theme "Sublimating daily life", Villette Makerz (external link)hosted an event co-organised with the French Craft Guild and the Agency Big Bang Project (external link) to enable a wide public to discover how artisans and designers inspire Nature to innovate and offer us useful, sustainable and aesthetics. Whether it's bioinspired, biomimetized, bioassisted projects and eco-responsible, linked to living systems approaches were highlighted, to put them back to the heart of any design. JEMAs are a great opportunity to raise awareness the environment by linking culture and education.
