In 2020, during the covid crisis, the THEMA Design agency reinvented itself by creating ARCHILIVE, the first digital exhibition dedicated to architecture, with showrooms and digital lectures. The two editions of ARCHILIVE brought together more than 1500 participants around two themes, "better living together" and "low carbon building".

body: >-


In a world affected by an unprecedented crisis, it is important to adapt to keep the link with your customers and prescribers! ARCHI'LIVE is:
An exhibition of virtual showrooms of companies
Personal business appointments
Live Lectures
This new industrial/architect meeting space aims to be an engine of economic revival that frees itself from borders and physical distancing. The French Craft was proud to present three of its members: Marine Bourgault, Maison Tercecret and i-Craft.
