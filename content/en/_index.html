Exceptional know-how X Innovation

body: >-


The French Craft Guild is an association under the law of 1901, working to bring together and promote French designers who combine know-how, creativity, innovation and design.

Enhancing France's creative heritage
French Craft supports the development and perpetuation of a culture of creativity and reinvention by (re)reconciling tradition and innovation.It encourages the hybridization of skills and talents by promoting and fostering the creative economy.It contributes to revealing and promoting French inventive genius through the excellence of the gesture and the reputation of its origin.It intends to contribute to the multiplication of the economic, image, societal and heritage values of the France brand.

Sénéquier
Our Manifesto
