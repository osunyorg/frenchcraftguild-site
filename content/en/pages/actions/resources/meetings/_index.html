Les Rencontres des Savoir-faire du Mobilier national
Dedicated to a wide audience, the "Rencontres des Savoir-faire" (Know-How Encounters) are weekly gatherings during which knowledge of design and craft creation, the history of art and decorative arts, and the activities of the Mobilier national and Manufactures nationales (Gobelins and Beauvais tapestries, Savonnerie carpets and Puy-en-Velay and Alençon lace) and its restoration workshops are shared.French Craft is pleased and proud to contribute to the program of the Rencontres des Savoir-faire.
