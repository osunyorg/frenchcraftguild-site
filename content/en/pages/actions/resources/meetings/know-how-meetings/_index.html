Autumn - winter 2024

On the agenda for this series of meetings: new sustainable materials.The Manufactures nationales has set up a Research and Innovation Department, in which the Sustainable Practices Laboratory is being developed. Several areas of applied research will be implemented. One of these involves support for the development and promotion of eco-responsible materials. These materials tend towards the same ideal of respect for the environment, and have several characteristics: they are renewable, biodegradable or recyclable, and non-toxic.This "Rencontre" is an opportunity to present and better understand the issues surrounding these materials, which are both innovative and at the heart of the challenges facing the craft industry.  > Materials from the sea: industries and value chains - Tuesday, November 26, 2024 at 6:30 pm with Thomas Eberhard (Fish leather), David Rosenblum (Atelier Bettenfeld-Rosenblum), Maxime Roux (Ostrea Design)  > Recovering value: the challenge of deposits - Tuesday December 17, 2024 at 6:30pm with Nativité Rodriguez (Atelier des Matières)

and Laurent Corio (Agence Les Nouvelles)  > Culture(s) materials - Tuesday January 28, 2025 at 6:30pm with Antonin Mongin, craftman-

textile designer, and Vincent Lartizien (Les Chanvres de l'Atlantique)

March - may 2024

On the program for this cycle: color.Without color, the decorative arts would not be a complete art: color is the soul and substance of the arts. And ever since Jehan Gobelin set up shop on the banks of the Bièvre at the end of the Middle Ages, the Mobilier National has been the institution where color takes on a special tone, at the heart of its textile creations and collections. This cycle takes stock of the latest research and achievements in the use of color in the arts and crafts.  > Color and senses: the state of the art - Tuesday November 28 at 7pm with 

Jean-Gabriel Causse, author in color, and Carole Calvez, olfactory designer 

Iris&Morphée.> Space and color. Perception and three-dimensionality - Tuesday, December 19 at 7pm with 

Pierre Gonalons, designer-decorator, and 

Sandrine Rozier textile designer-costumière.> Nature Rainbow: the materiality of color - Tuesday, January 30 at 7pm with 

Guillaume Millot, art weaver, and 

Caroline Besse, artist and painter-decorator.

March - june 2023

In this series of encounters, discover those who bring together a diversity of design professions and practices, between reflection, tradition and innovation, creation, materials and societal issues.> Nature as source and inspiration - Tuesday March 14 at 6pm with Guillian Graves, designer, 

Agence Big Bang Project and Violaine Buet, artisan-designer, La manufacture des algues.> Art and craft: theoretical issues

- Tuesday April 11 at 6pm with Aurélien Fouillet, researcher, and Fabrice Pincin, designer-teacher-researcher.> Territories and development through arts and crafts - Tuesday, May 16 at 6pm with Tom Hébrard, designer, Tiers-lieu la Martinière ; Ariane Vitou, Fonds de dotation Terre & Fils and Anne-Claire Duprat, Fondation Martell.

On the agenda for this series of meetings: new sustainable materials.

This "Rencontre" is an opportunity to present and better understand the issues surrounding these materials, which are both innovative and at the heart of the challenges facing the craft industry.
